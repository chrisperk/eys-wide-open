At a Glance

The OpenEYS Project is a people-driven project to deliver change to those suffering retinitis pigmentosa type 25 and possibly other forms of blindness. Done by means of protein replacement therapy of the EYS (EYes Shut) gene (a gene with regenerative properties that maintains the health of your eyes). The plan is to grow copies in a cell culture and then inject the cure not unlike how a diabetic injects insulin to stabilize their blood sugar levels.
